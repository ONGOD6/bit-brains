# EIP-0003 — Supply Cap and Emission Constraints

## Abstract

This document defines the supply constraints for the Bit Brains ecosystem collections.

---

## Supply Limits

The Pickle Punks NFT collection has a maximum supply of:

1500 NFTs

The Bit Brains NFT collection has a maximum supply of:

1500 NFTs

---

## Minting Constraints

The smart contracts responsible for minting each collection must enforce the supply limits.

No tokens may be minted beyond the defined supply cap.

---

## Finality

Once deployed, these supply limits cannot be modified.
